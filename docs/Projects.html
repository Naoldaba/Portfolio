Project Overview

Abronet.org is a specific Fintech platform that aims to modernize and digitize the traditional Equb concept, providing a digital platform for individuals to participate in similar group savings and lending activities.

An Equb, also known as an Iqub or Hundi, is a traditional rotating savings and credit association found in various cultures around the world. In an Equb, a group of individuals contribute a fixed amount of money at regular intervals (e.g., weekly or monthly).

The total sum is then given to one member of the group in a cycle, and this process repeats until all members have received their share. Equbs provide a way for people to save and access capital in communities where formal banking services may be limited.
